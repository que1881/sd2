Review On My Business

Cleanserve is a social savings club that allows parents to save towards their kids' education. The system aims to reward frequent savers with vouchers redeemable only towards the purchase of textbooks/stationery items for their kids.

Objectives

The main objective of the system is to save money through the platform for children's future educational needs.
